    Principles

    "A man must have a Code."

    • 1. Love what you make.

      Designing is not just a job, it’s my life’s work. I do this because i love it. This passion and investment is what drives me towards creating truly great products.

    • 2. Uncompromising Quality.

      Quality is a relentless pursuit of craftsmanship that i put into every detail of my work. Quality is how i ensure that my work creates meaning and value.

    • 3. Perfection doesn’t do 9-­5.

      And neither do I. I’m not bound to any time zones. The project dictates where I need to be and who I need to collaborate with. I always go the extra mile.

    • 4. Creator. Partner. Collaborator.

      Ideas are far from linear. As designer and developer, I can spearhead, support or polish your project or product at any stage it needs it.

    • 5. Clean ­– In thinking, in style.

      Simple, minimal and effective. This is my mantra and method. Clean code, fuss­free interfaces and expert execution, design as it should be.

    • 6. UX is the sum of all things.

      There’s no style over substance here. I bring together form and the function to create something that looks good and performs brilliantly. Simple.
